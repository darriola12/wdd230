Attractions

  • Canopy Tour: Soar above the coastline and take in breathtaking views of the ocean and surrounding landscape as you zip through the air on our thrilling canopy tour. (Time slots available throughout the day, booking required)
  • Surfing Lessons Hang ten and catch some waves with expert instructors who will guide you through the basics of surfing. Whether you're a beginner or seasoned pro, there's no better place to catch a wave than at our event. (Multiple sessions available, equipment provided)
  • Horseback Riding Tours: Experience the beauty of the beach from a different perspective with our guided horseback riding tours. Trot along the shoreline and explore hidden coves as you create unforgettable memories with family and friends. (Scheduled tours throughout the day, limited spots available)
  • Jet Ski Rentals Feel the adrenaline rush as you speed across the water on your very own jet ski! Rent a jet ski and explore the open sea at your own pace or join one of our guided tours for an exhilarating adventure. (Hourly rentals available, must be 18 or older with valid ID)
